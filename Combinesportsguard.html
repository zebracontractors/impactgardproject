How It Works

STEP 1 Digital Scanning

We take a detailed digital scan of your teeth and mouth to ensure a precise, custom-fit mouthguard that offers maximum comfort and protection.

STEP 2 Custom Design

Our experts design the mouthguard based on your scan, adjusting thickness and material to meet your specific needs.

STEP 3 3D Printing

An accurate mold is created using advanced 3D printing technology, ensuring a perfect fit.

STEP 4 Fabrication

We manufacture the mouthguard from high-quality materials, layering to achieve the ideal thickness and durability.

STEP 5 Delivered Straight to Your Home

After finalizing with finishing touches and a quality check, your custom mouthguard is delivered to your home. Optional final fitting available!
