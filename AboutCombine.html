About Us

At ImpactGard, we are just getting started on a mission to protect athletes and enhance their performance. Founded by a dentist, dental lab owner, and coach with a passion for sports, ImpactGard is focused on creating high-quality, customizable mouthguards. Drawing from expertise in dentistry, lab craftsmanship, and sports, we aim to provide athletes with the ultimate protection and comfort. Though we’re new, our commitment to safety and performance is at the core of everything we do.

Our Mission

Our mission is to redefine athlete protection through cutting-edge technology. We leverage advanced 3D scanning and 3D technology to create mouthguards that offer a custom-fit and unparalleled comfort. By integrating precision craftsmanship with innovative tech, we ensure every athlete receives a mouthguard that is perfectly tailored to their unique needs. Our goal is to enhance performance and safety, empowering athletes to compete with confidence and reach their full potential.
